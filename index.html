A Bit About Me

At first I started learning how to code purely by curiosity. I'm driven by things I don't understand, that's why I thrive on learning new stuff all the time.

I've recently graduated in Electrical Engineering in Brazil. It was a 5 year college, when I focused my spare time into learning everything I could about Financial Markets, once I was hypnotized by the siren song of the rich wannabes.

Until I finally started a summer internship at a brokerage firm that was about to become the largest one for Individuals in Brazil. By that time they were expanding their business to NYC and then I got an exciting opportunity to work at the International Desk. However, as exciting I thought the subject was, my experience there weren't so great. The office culture was so futile and all I could hear about all day long was how people made money fast and how they spent it even faster! My excitement slowly faded away and I decided to try something else...
